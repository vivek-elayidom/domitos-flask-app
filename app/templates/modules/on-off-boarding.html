Onboarding & Offboarding Module

On-boarding to off-boarding module (ONFB) is designed to handle all the functionalities of the clients and tenants during the lifecycle of their contract. This includes managing the client, contracted units, tenants, service requests raised from the client and payment notices against rendering such services. Facilities and their administrators will be able to manage their daily client operations entirely using this module.

  • Client & Contract Management
  • Data Access Management
  • Templated quotes and services
  • Unit Management
  • Tenants Management
  • Service Request
  • Unit availability & reservation
  • Payment Notice
  • Query Search
  • Incident Management
  • Amenity Booking
  • Incident Reporting
  • Reports
