Maintenance Management

Property maintenance module handles functionalities related to maintenance of your facilities through preventive maintenance, inspections, issue registration and resolution, work orders for employees etc.

  • Create and manage preventive maintenance lists
  • System automatically tracks completed maintenance activities schedules next depending on the periodicity
  • Generate work orders for your staff to attend to maintenance item
  • Set deadline for completion of Work Orders
  • Application automatically notifies respective vendors if maintenance is to be handled by third party service providers
  • Ability for workers to attach completion pictures to remotely ensure work is completed and recorded in the system for audit as well
