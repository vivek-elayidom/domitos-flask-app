Proposals and Contract Management

Domitos is designed to handle all the functionalities of the clients and manage all contract related information during the lifecycle of their contract.

  • View all activities conducted related to the client
  • Manage contact information and client self-service portal All contract related information
  • Centralized contract management
  • Ability to upload contacts into the system with automated tracking on contract expiry
  • In built system capability to extend or prematurely terminate contracts
  • Ability to amend contracts in case client decides to change units or services rendered.
  • View pricing history offered to the client. This can be used to manage price information during renewal negotiations.
